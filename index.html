Optimizing…

Raven's Dox2 Trait Planner

// Overview

This planner helps you optimize your creature's trait loadout to hit the 75% Damage Reduction (DR) cap, then squeeze out as much Damage and Speed as possible with your remaining trait slots.

Trait power is shown as both a multiplier (what you see on the buffs screen) and a percentage (what you see mounted). They mean the same thing — 1.20 = 20%.

// Recommended Workflow

  1. Set your minimum speed. Decide the lowest Speed % you're willing to accept on your mount and enter it in the Minimum Speed field. The optimizer will always try to meet this before maximizing damage.
  2. Set exclusions. If you don't own certain traits yet, select them in the Exclusions section so the optimizer won't suggest them.
  3. Feed the Corrupt String (optional). If you choose to use a Corrupt String, select which stat it affected and enter the bonus % it gave. Be aware — this carries risk in-game.
  4. Feed the Greater Strings (optional). Enter the stat and bonus % for up to 3 Greater Strings you've fed your creature.
  5. Select a Mega Trait (optional). Mega traits have powerful secondary effects beyond their raw stat bonus and are often a major build decision. Choose yours before optimizing so the planner accounts for it.
  6. Select a Legendary Trait (optional). Legendary traits are powerful but come with negative modifiers — feed it early so later traits can offset the downsides. Select it here if you plan to use one.
  7. Hit Optimize. The planner will fill your remaining slots with the best combination of traits to hit 75% DR first, then meet your speed requirement, then maximize damage.

// Reading the Results

The floating bar at the top always shows your current totals. DR shown is capped at 75% in the display even if your traits exceed it — going over the cap is fine and sometimes unavoidable.
